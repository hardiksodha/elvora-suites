Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury in the City of Joy

Nestled in the cultural heartbeat of Kolkata, Elvora Suites has been a sanctuary for discerning travellers since its founding. We blend the warmth of Bengali hospitality with the elegance of modern luxury.

From our grand colonial-inspired architecture to our thoughtfully designed suites, every corner of Elvora tells a story — one of heritage, artistry, and genuine care.
