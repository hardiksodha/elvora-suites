Our Story

Elvora Suites – Hotel in Bhowanipore, Kolkata

A Boutique Hotel Born from Kolkata's Spirit

Elvora Suites is a soon-to-open boutique hotel nestled in Bhowanipore — one of South Kolkata's most vibrant and culturally rich neighbourhoods. We are designed for travellers who value comfort, character, and a genuine connection to the city.

Thoughtfully designed rooms, warm service, and a love for Kolkata's energy — that is the promise of Elvora Suites. Whether you are here for business or leisure, we will make it feel like home.

Elvora Suites is a premium hotel in Bhowanipore, Kolkata offering comfortable and budget-friendly rooms for families and business travelers. Conveniently located near Kalighat Temple, Park Street, and major South Kolkata attractions.
