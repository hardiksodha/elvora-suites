Our Story

A Boutique Hotel Born from Kolkata's Spirit

Elvora Suites is a soon-to-open boutique hotel nestled in Bhavanipur — one of South Kolkata's most vibrant and culturally rich neighbourhoods. We are designed for travellers who value comfort, character, and a genuine connection to the city.

Thoughtfully designed rooms, warm service, and a love for Kolkata's energy — that is the promise of Elvora Suites. Whether you are here for business or leisure, we will make it feel like home.
